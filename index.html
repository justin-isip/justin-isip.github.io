About

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher in the Biodiversity Futures Lab at the Natural History Museum in London. My research focuses on how anthropogenic threats impact biodiveristy on a global scale, with a particular focus on insects. I work mainly with the PREDICTS database and I'm also embedded within the NERC-funded project GLiTRS.

Research interests

  • Biodiversity informatics
  • Species distribution modelling
  • Climate change and extinction risk
  • Machine learning for ecology
  • Global biodiversity databases
