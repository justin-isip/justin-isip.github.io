About

I'm Justin, a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Biodiversity Futures Lab at the Natural History Museum in London working on the NERC-funded GLiTRS project. I recently completed my PHD at the Natural History Museum and the Centre for Biodiversity and Environmental Research (CBER) at UCL supervised by Professor Andy Purvis and Professor Tim Newbold. My PhD research used the PREDICTS database to understand how land-use change and intensification reorganise and restructure insect communities around the world. Outside of research, I'm also passionate about making ecology a more diverse and inclusive field. I sit on the committee of the REED Network working to develop greater cross-pollination between EDI networks and initiatives both within and outside of academia.
